# Getting Started with Vibe CRM

Welcome to Vibe CRM! This guide will help you get up and running in just a few minutes.

## What is Vibe CRM?

Vibe CRM is a customer relationship management system designed to help you:
- Manage client relationships
- Track projects and their progress
- Organize tasks and work assignments
- Create and track invoices
- Manage your team

## First Login

### 1. Access Vibe CRM

Navigate to your Vibe CRM instance in your web browser.

### 2. Log In

Enter your email address and password on the login screen.

If you don't have an account, contact your organization administrator to invite you.

### 3. You're In!

After logging in, you'll see the Dashboard with an overview of your work.

## Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard shows you a quick snapshot of your organization:

- **Statistics Cards** - Number of clients, projects, tasks, and invoices
- **Revenue Chart** - Historical revenue from invoices
- **Recent Activity** - Latest changes in your system

### Your First Steps

1. Go to **Clients** - See your existing clients or add a new one
2. Go to **Projects** - Create a project for a client
3. Go to **Tasks** - Break down project work into tasks
4. Go to **Invoices** - Generate an invoice for completed work
5. Go to **Team** - Invite team members

## Main Features

### Managing Clients

**View Clients**
1. Click **Clients** in the sidebar
2. See all clients in a table view
3. Click a client to view details

**Add a New Client**
1. Click the **New Client** button
2. Fill in client information:
   - Name (required)
   - Email (required)
   - Phone (optional)
   - Address (optional)
3. Click **Create**

**Edit a Client**
1. Find the client in the list
2. Click **Edit** or click the client row
3. Update information
4. Click **Save**

**Delete a Client**
1. Find the client in the list
2. Click **Delete**
3. Confirm deletion

### Managing Projects

**View Projects**
1. Click **Projects** in the sidebar
2. See all projects with their status and priority
3. Filter by status or priority if needed

**Create a Project**
1. Click the **New Project** button
2. Fill in project details:
   - Name (required)
   - Description (optional)
   - Select a client (optional)
   - Set status (Backlog, Active, Completed, On Hold)
   - Set priority (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
3. Click **Create**

Projects help you organize work for clients and track overall progress.

### Organizing with Tasks

**View Task Board**
1. Click **Tasks** in the sidebar
2. See tasks organized in columns by status
3. Status columns: Backlog, To Do, In Progress, Review, Done

**Create a Task**
1. Click **New Task** or click "+" in any status column
2. Fill in task details:
   - Title (required)
   - Description (optional)
   - Project (select one)
   - Status (In Progress, etc.)
   - Priority (Low to Urgent)
   - Assign to a team member
   - Due date (optional)
3. Click **Create**

**Move a Task**
Drag and drop tasks between columns to change status:
- **Backlog** - Ideas not yet started
- **To Do** - Upcoming work
- **In Progress** - Currently being worked on
- **Review** - Waiting for review
- **Done** - Completed

**Update a Task**
1. Click a task to open it
2. Update any field (title, description, assignee, due date)
3. Changes save automatically

**Complete a Task**
1. Drag the task to the **Done** column, or
2. Open the task and change status to "Done"

### Creating Invoices

**View Invoices**
1. Click **Invoices** in the sidebar
2. See all invoices with their status and amounts
3. Filter by status (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue)

**Create an Invoice**
1. Click **New Invoice**
2. Select the client
3. Set due date
4. Add line items (what you're charging for):
   - Item description
   - Quantity
   - Unit price
   - Tax rate (optional)
5. Click **Create Invoice**

The invoice total calculates automatically.

**Send an Invoice**
1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Mark as Sent**
3. The status changes to "Sent" and date is recorded

**Mark as Paid**
1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Mark as Paid**
3. The status changes to "Paid"

The revenue is then reflected in your dashboard charts.

**Download Invoice**
1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. A PDF file downloads to your computer

### Team Management

**View Team Members**
1. Click **Users** in the sidebar
2. See all team members in your organization
3. See their role and status

**Invite a Team Member**
1. Click **Invite User**
2. Enter their email address
3. Select their role:
   - **Member** - Can view and work on assigned items
   - **Admin** - Can also manage team members
   - **Owner** - Full access to organization
4. Click **Invite**

An invitation is sent to their email.

**Change a Team Member's Role**
1. Find the member in the list
2. Click **Edit**
3. Change their role
4. Click **Save**

**Remove a Team Member**
1. Find the member in the list
2. Click **Remove**
3. Confirm removal

## Organization Settings

If you're an organization owner or admin:

1. Click **Organizations** in the sidebar
2. Manage organization settings
3. View and manage team members
4. Configure billing and plan

Most list pages have a search box at the top:
1. Type to filter results

If you're a member of multiple organizations:
1. Click your avatar in the top right

3. Dashboard updates to show that organization's data

To enable dark mode:
1. Click your avatar in the top right
2. Click **Settings**

## Common Workflows

### Onboarding a New Client

1. Create the client (Clients page)
2. Create a project for the client (Projects page)
3. Break down work into tasks (Tasks page)
4. Assign tasks to team members (Tasks page)
5. Move tasks through statuses as work progresses
6. Create invoice when work is complete (Invoices page)
7. Mark invoice as sent when sent to client
8. Mark invoice as paid when payment received

### Managing a Project

1. Go to Projects page
2. Find the project
3. View associated tasks (click project name)
4. Create new tasks as needed
5. Assign tasks to team members
6. Move tasks through workflow as work progresses
7. Mark project complete when all tasks done

### Tracking Invoice Payment

1. Go to Invoices page
2. Create invoice for work completed
3. Click **Send** when sending to client (updates status)
4. Click **Paid** when payment is received
5. Revenue appears in dashboard charts
